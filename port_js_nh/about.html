It didn't take long after relocating to Boulder, to realize that everything is better out west. Since discovering the great backyard that Colorado has to offer, I've been fortunate to work with a variety of clients and companies exposing myself to a wide range of projects and techniques. These experiences have allowed me to develop a diverse skillset and applying this background to the details and craftsmanship of my work is something that I strive for daily.

I'm a detail-oriented and proficient creative with over 10 years experience working with brands ranging from global products to local services. I possess reliable collaborative and interpersonal skills while working with a team, yet exhibit strong self-management of time and workload. Developing relationships and working with clients and vendors is something that I truly enjoy and is crucial to the success of every project.

My creative pursuits range from product catalog design and production, to website design and brand identity development. For me, all ideas initiate through pen or pencil and that's why I always have my sketchbook close by. Experimenting with watercolor, pen and ink drawings and photography are other creative process that I enjoy, as well as creating handmade typography and calligraphy, which has become a very therapeutic way for me to spend my time.
